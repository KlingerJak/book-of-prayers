chapter: Nephi's Prayer for Deliverance

chapter-sum: Which he prayed after being bound with strong cords by his brethers, after reprimanding them for their rebellion.
chapter-sum: As recorded in 1 Nephi 7:17 OPV
chapter-sum: Or 1 Nephi 2:28-29 RAV

. O Lord, according to my faith which is in thee, wilt thou deliver me from the hands of my brethren, yea, even give me strength that I may burst these bands with which I am bound.
