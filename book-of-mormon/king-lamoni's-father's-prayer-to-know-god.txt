chapter: King Lamoni's Father's Prayer to Know God

chapter-sum: Which he prayed after hearing the teachings and testimony of Aaron.
chapter-sum: As recorded in Alma 22:18 OPV
chapter-sum: Or Alma 13:52-53 RAV

. O God, 

. Aaron hath told me that there is a God.

. And if there is a God, and if thou art God, wilt thou make thyself known unto me?

. And I will give away all my sins to know thee and that I may be raised from the dead and be saved at the last day.
