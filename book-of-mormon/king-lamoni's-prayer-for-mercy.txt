chapter: King Lamoni's Prayer for Mercy

chapter-sum: Which he prayed after accepting Jesus Christ through the testimony of Ammon.
chapter-sum: As recorded in Alma 18:41 OPV
chapter-sum: Or Alma 12:122 RAV

. O Lord, have mercy; according to thy abundant mercy which thou hast had upon the people of Nephi, have upon me and my people.
