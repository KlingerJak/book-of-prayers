chapter: Enos's Prayer About Redemption

chapter-sum: Which he prayed after the voice of the Lord granted forgiveness to him.
chapter-sum: As recorded in Enos 1:7 OPV
chapter-sum: Or Enos 1:9 RAV

subchapter: Enos -
. Lord, how is it done?

subchapter: The Lord - 
. Because of thy faith in Christ, whom thou hast never before heard nor seen. And many years pass away before he shall manifest himself in the flesh; wherefore, go to; thy faith hath made thee whole.
